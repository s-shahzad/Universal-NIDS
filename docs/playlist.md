# NIDS Offering Playlist

Updated: 2026-03-07

## Service Expansion Items

1. SOC-Lite Service
- 24/7 monitoring, alert triage, weekly incident summaries.

2. Industry Packs
- Prebuilt EVCS, IoT, healthcare, and enterprise detection profiles.

3. Automated Response
- Optional auto-block, quarantine, and suppression workflows.

4. Compliance Reporting
- Audit-ready reports for controls, incidents, and retention history.

5. Threat Intel Enrichment
- IOC/reputation enrichment with prioritized alerting.

6. Incident Workflow Add-on
- Assignment, status tracking, escalation, and SLA dashboard.

7. Deployment Offering
- On-prem, cloud, Docker/K8s setup and hardening.

8. Training and Playbooks
- Team runbooks, tabletop simulations, and response SOPs.
